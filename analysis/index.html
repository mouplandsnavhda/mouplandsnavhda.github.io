Progeny UT scores

Here is how to interpret the tables below - Using (breeder)Clyde Vetter as an example. He was listed as the breeder on 88 dogs that have ran utility. Those 88 dogs had a combined 106 Prize 1 scores, 30 Prize 2 scores, 12 Prize 3 scores, and 12 Prize None scores. 106 Prize 1 scores is 66% of all the Prize 1,2,3,and None combined. Remember, dogs can run the utility test many times. If a given dog runs 3 times, and gets 3 Prize 1's, then the Prize 1 score will be incremented 3 times. Also, if a given dog attempts to attain a prize 1 by running over and over, it will be reflected in the percentages. Click on the headers to sort if you're curious
